# What is WWW? 

WWW stands for World Wide Web.

It is a system of websites and web pages that you can access through the Internet using a web browser.

** Example **

 www.google.com

 www.wikipedia.org 

 | The Internet is the network that connects computers, while the WWW is a service that runs on the Internet and lets you access websites.|
